What is the Technology Student Association



The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national, non-profit organization of more than 250,000 middle and high school students engaged in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Almost 4,000,000 student members have participated in TSA since 1978 through challenging competitions, leadership opportunities, and community service.
